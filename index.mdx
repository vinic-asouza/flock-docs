Flock is a SaaS church management platform built for church administrators who need a modern, reliable way to manage their congregation. Whether you oversee a single meeting point or a multi-site church, Flock brings together member records, integration pipelines, congregations, groups, a calendar, and rich analytics — all in one secure, responsive web app. You don't need to juggle spreadsheets or disconnected tools; everything your team needs lives in Flock.

## How to get started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Go to the Flock registration page and fill in your church name, contact details, and your personal credentials. You'll receive a confirmation email — click the link to activate your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up your church profile">
    After confirming your email, select a plan (free for up to 100 members) and complete your church's profile: denomination, address, and contact information. This takes less than two minutes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add your members">
    Navigate to **Membros** in the sidebar and start adding your congregation. You can add members one by one, import a CSV file in bulk, or share a public self-registration link so members can fill in their own details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore reports and analytics">
    Click **Painel** (the first item in the sidebar) to see your analytics dashboard. View demographic breakdowns, growth trends, congregation-by-congregation stats, and export a full PDF report for your leadership.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Flock's free plan supports up to **100 active members** — no credit card required. If your church grows beyond that, you can upgrade to a paid plan at any time from **Settings → Subscription**. See the [Plans & Billing](/plans-billing) page for current pricing and feature comparisons.
</Note>
