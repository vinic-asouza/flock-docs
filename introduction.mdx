Flock is a modern, cloud-based church management platform designed to replace the spreadsheets, paper rosters, and disconnected apps that slow churches down. It gives you a single place to maintain accurate membership records, track visitors moving toward formal membership, organize your congregations and groups, manage your church calendar, and understand your community through detailed analytics. Because Flock is a responsive web application, you and your team can access it from any device — desktop, tablet, or phone — without installing anything.

## Your church's data stays private

Flock is built on a **multi-tenant architecture**, which means every church operates in a completely isolated silo. Your membership data, congregations, groups, and reports are never visible to other churches using the platform. Each request is authenticated and tied to your church's unique identifier, so there is no way for data to leak across accounts.

## Role-based access control

Not everyone on your team needs full administrative access. Flock lets you invite team members at four access levels:

| Role | What they can do |
|------|-----------------|
| **Reader** | View all data — members, groups, congregations, reports |
| **Editor** | Everything a Reader can do, plus create, edit, and delete members, groups, congregations, and calendar events |
| **Admin** | Everything an Editor can do, plus manage church settings, invite users, view audit logs, and manage the subscription |
| **Owner** | Full access, including sensitive account operations such as changing the master email, deleting the account, and all admin actions |

You can manage your team's roles at any time from **Settings → Users**.

## Works on any device

Flock is a responsive web application built with modern frontend technology. Whether you're at your office desk, checking records from a tablet during a service, or reviewing a report on your phone, the interface adapts to your screen size. There is nothing to install — just open your browser and log in.

## Subscription plans

Flock is designed to grow with your church. You start for free and upgrade only when you need more capacity.

| Plan | Member limit | Price |
|------|-------------|-------|
| Free | 100 members | R$ 0 |
| Basic | 200 members | R$ 29,99/month |
| Standard | 500 members | R$ 59,99/month |
| Advanced | 800 members | R$ 89,99/month |

The **member counter** is always visible at the top of the app so you know exactly where you stand. Inactive members remain in your system for historical records but do not count against your plan limit. When you're ready to upgrade, go to **Settings → Subscription**.

<Note>
  For churches with more than 800 members, contact the Flock support team to discuss a custom plan tailored to your needs.
</Note>
