Audit logs give you a complete, tamper-proof record of every important action performed in your Flock account. Any time a member is created or deleted, a congregation is edited, a user role changes, or any other significant operation occurs, Flock automatically creates a log entry. This gives church leadership full visibility and accountability over how the system is being used — and by whom.

## Who Can View Audit Logs

Only users with the **Owner** or **Admin** role can access audit logs. Editor and Reader users do not have access to this section.

## Accessing Audit Logs

Navigate to **Configurações** in the sidebar, then click the **Logs** tab at the top of the Settings page.

## What Is Logged

Flock records log entries for all of the following operations:

| Entity | Logged Actions |
|---|---|
| **Members** | Created, updated (including field-level changes), activated, deactivated, deleted |
| **Integrantes** | Created, updated, deleted, converted to member |
| **Congregations** | Created, updated, deleted |
| **Groups** | Created, updated, deleted |
| **Calendar Events** | Created, updated, deleted |
| **Church Profile** | Updated (name, CNPJ, address, contacts) |
| **Account** | Email changed, password changed, account deleted |

## What Each Log Entry Shows

Every log entry in Flock contains the following information:

- **Entity type** — What was affected (e.g., Member, Congregation, Church)
- **Action** — What happened (Created, Updated, Activated, Deactivated, Deleted)
- **Record name** — The name or identifier of the affected record
- **Timestamp** — The exact date and time the action was performed (displayed in Brazilian date/time format)
- **Before & after values** — For update actions, Flock shows exactly which fields changed and what the old and new values were

Click **Ver detalhes** on any log entry to expand it and see the full before/after comparison.

## Filtering Logs

You can narrow down the log history using the filter controls at the top of the Logs page:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Filter by Entity" icon="layer-group">
    Show only logs for a specific area of the system: Members, Church, Account, Congregations, Groups, Calendar, or Integração.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Filter by Action" icon="sliders">
    Show only a specific type of action: Criação (create), Atualização (update), Ativação (activate), Inativação (deactivate), or Exclusão (delete).
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

To reset to the full log view, set both filters back to "Todas" (all).

## Pagination

Audit logs are paginated — by default, 10 entries are shown per page. Use the **Anterior** and **Próximo** buttons at the bottom of the log list to navigate through older records. The page indicator shows your current position (e.g., "Página 2 de 14") and the total number of entries matching your current filter.

<Tip>
  Review your audit logs periodically — for example, once a week — to confirm that only authorized changes are being made to your member records and church data. This is especially useful if multiple staff members have Editor or Admin access.
</Tip>

<Note>
  Audit log entries **cannot be edited or deleted** — not even by the Owner. This ensures the integrity of your activity history and provides a reliable trail for accountability purposes.
</Note>
