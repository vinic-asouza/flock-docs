Your church profile is the core identity of your organization in Flock. It stores your official name, legal registration details, physical address, and contact information — all of which appear throughout the platform and in exported documents. Keeping this information accurate ensures that your reports, member PDFs, and administrative records always reflect your church correctly.

## What You Can Update

From the Church Profile settings you can edit the following fields:

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| **Church Name** | The official name of your church. Appears throughout the app and in exported PDFs. |
| **Denomination** | Your church's denomination or theological stream (e.g., Assembleia de Deus, Batista). |
| **CNPJ** | Your church's Brazilian tax registration number. Validated automatically. |
| **Street** | Street name for your church's main address. |
| **Number** | Street number. |
| **Complement** | Apartment, suite, or additional address details. |
| **City** | City where your church is located. |
| **State** | Brazilian state (UF). |
| **Email** | Official contact email for your church. |
| **Phone** | Main phone number. |
| **WhatsApp** | WhatsApp contact number. |

## Where to Find It

Navigate to **Configurações** in the left sidebar, then select the **Igreja** tab at the top of the settings page.

## How to Update Your Church Profile

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    Click **Configurações** in the sidebar navigation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the Igreja tab">
    At the top of the Settings page, click the **Igreja** tab to open the Church Profile section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit your information">
    Click the **Editar** button or click directly into any field you want to update. Fill in the new values for the fields you want to change.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save your changes">
    Click **Salvar** to apply the updates. You'll see a confirmation message when the changes have been saved successfully.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## CNPJ Validation

Flock automatically validates your CNPJ when you save the profile. The validation checks both the format (14 digits) and the verification digits against the official algorithm. If the CNPJ is invalid, you'll see an error message and the form will not be saved until a valid number is entered.

<Note>
  Each CNPJ can only be registered once in Flock. If you enter a CNPJ that is already associated with another church account, you will see an error indicating that the CNPJ is already in use. Contact support if you believe this is an error.
</Note>

## Permissions

Updating church profile information requires at least **Admin** role. Users with **Editor** or **Reader** roles can view the church profile but cannot save changes.

<Note>
  Your church name appears in the sidebar, on the dashboard, and on all exported PDFs — including individual member records, member list exports, and dashboard reports. Make sure the name is entered exactly as you want it to appear on official documents.
</Note>
