Flock uses **Stripe** for secure payment processing, so your financial data is never stored directly in Flock. All billing actions — upgrading, downgrading, viewing invoices, and updating your credit card — are managed from **Configurações → Plano**. Only the account **Owner** can access billing settings.

## Available Plans

| Plan | Member Limit | Monthly Price |
|---|:---:|:---:|
| **Free** | 100 members | R$ 0 |
| **200 Members** | 200 members | R$ 29,99/month |
| **500 Members** | 500 members | R$ 59,99/month |
| **800 Members** | 800 members | R$ 89,99/month |

All paid plans include every feature in Flock. The only difference between plans is the maximum number of active members you can have in your account.

## Viewing Your Current Plan

When you open **Configurações → Plano**, you'll see a summary card showing:

- Your **current plan** name and price
- Your subscription **status** (Active, Cancelled, Past Due, etc.)
- The subscription **start date**
- Any upcoming changes (e.g., a scheduled end date if you've cancelled)

## Upgrading Your Plan

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Settings → Plano">
    Click **Configurações** in the sidebar, then select the **Plano** tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Trocar de Plano">
    Click the **Trocar de Plano** button. This button is only active when your subscription is in an Active state.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your new plan">
    A modal will appear listing all available plans. Click the plan you want to upgrade to. Your current plan will be marked and cannot be selected.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm the change">
    Review the confirmation screen showing your current plan and your new plan, then click **Confirmar Troca**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Change takes effect immediately">
    Your new member limit is active right away. Stripe will calculate a prorated charge for the remaining days in your current billing period and apply it to your next invoice.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Downgrading Your Plan

Downgrading follows the same steps as upgrading — open **Trocar de Plano**, select a lower-tier plan, and confirm. The change takes effect immediately.

<Warning>
  If your church currently has more active members than the new plan allows, Flock will **block the downgrade** until you reduce your active member count to fit within the new limit. For example, if you have 350 active members and want to downgrade to the 200-member plan, you'll need to deactivate at least 150 members first. Flock will show you exactly how many members you need to remove before the change can proceed.
</Warning>

## Accessing the Stripe Billing Portal

The Stripe Billing Portal gives you full control over your payment details and invoice history. From it you can:

- View and download **past invoices**
- Update your **credit card** or payment method
- **Cancel** your subscription

To open the portal:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Settings → Plano">
    Click **Configurações** in the sidebar, then select the **Plano** tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Gerenciar Assinatura">
    Click the **Gerenciar Assinatura** button. The Stripe portal will open in a new tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Make your changes in Stripe">
    Update your payment method, download invoices, or cancel your subscription directly in the Stripe portal.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Return to Flock">
    When you're done, close the Stripe tab and return to Flock. Your subscription data will refresh automatically when you navigate back to the **Plano** tab.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What Happens When You Reach Your Member Limit

Flock monitors your active member count against your plan limit at all times.

- As you approach your limit, you'll see a **warning** on the member list and dashboard.
- Once you reach the limit, **adding new members will be blocked**. The "add member" action will show an error explaining that you've reached your plan's limit.
- To continue adding members, go to **Configurações → Plano** and upgrade to a higher plan.

<Note>
  Only **active members** count toward your plan limit. Inactive members and integrantes (people in the integration/onboarding process) do not affect your count.
</Note>

## Cancelling Your Subscription

To cancel, open the **Stripe Billing Portal** (via **Gerenciar Assinatura**) and cancel from there.

- After cancelling, you'll keep full access to your paid plan until the **end of the current billing period**.
- Once the billing period ends, your account automatically reverts to the **Free plan** (100-member limit).
- If you have more than 100 active members when the downgrade occurs, you won't be able to add new members until you reduce your active count below 100.

## Payment Issues

If your payment fails, your subscription will enter a **Past Due** state. You'll see a prominent alert on the **Plano** page prompting you to update your payment method. New members cannot be added while a payment is overdue. Click **Atualizar pagamento** in the alert to open the Stripe portal and resolve the issue.

<Note>
  Only the account **Owner** can view or manage billing, subscription, and payment settings. Admin, Editor, and Reader users will not see the **Plano** tab in Settings.
</Note>
