The Integration module is a purpose-built pipeline for people who are part of your church community but are not yet full members — first-time visitors, new attendees going through a discipleship class, or candidates preparing for baptism and membership. Instead of cluttering your member list with provisional records, Flock keeps these people in a separate, dedicated space where you can track their journey, assign a mentor to walk alongside them, and convert them to full members with a single action when the time comes.

## What is an "integrante"?

An **integrante** (integration candidate) is someone who:

- Visited your church and expressed interest in membership
- Is currently attending a membership preparation class
- Is in an active discipleship or follow-up process with a church leader

Integrantes are distinct from full members. They have their own list, their own status workflow, and their own export options. Critically, they do **not** consume a slot on your plan's member limit — that only happens when they are converted.

<Tip>
  Register anyone attending your church for the first time as an integrante rather than a member. This keeps your member list accurate while still letting you track and care for new people from day one.
</Tip>

## Creating an integrante

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Integration module">
    Click **Integração** in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a new integrante">
    Click **Adicionar integrante**. The form opens.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in the details">
    Enter the person's name (required), date of birth, phone number, email, and any other contact information you have available.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set congregation and mentor">
    Select the **congregação esperada** (the congregation they are likely to join) and, if you already know who will be walking alongside them, assign a **mentor** from your existing member list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Salvar**. The integrante appears in the list with status **Em andamento**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Integration status

Every integrante moves through one of three statuses:

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Em andamento" icon="hourglass-half" href="#">
    The default status. The person is actively in the integration process — being visited, attending classes, or in regular contact with their mentor.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Convertido" icon="check-circle" href="#">
    The integrante has been converted to a full member. Their record is closed in the integration pipeline and a new member profile exists in the Members module.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Descartado" icon="circle-xmark" href="#">
    The process was closed without conversion — the person stopped attending or decided not to pursue membership. Their record is preserved for historical reference.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Mentor assignment

Assigning a mentor ties an existing church member to the integrante's journey. The mentor's name appears on the integrante's record and is available as a filter, making it easy for leaders to see which mentors have active cases and how many people each one is accompanying.

You can reassign the mentor at any time by editing the integrante's profile.

## Converting to a full member

When an integrante is ready for full membership, convert them directly from their profile:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the integrante's record">
    Find the person in the Integration list and click their name to open their details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click 'Converter para membro'">
    This opens a pre-filled member registration form with the integrante's data already populated.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete the member profile">
    Review and supplement the information — add baptism date, official congregation, role, and any other fields required for a full member record.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Salvar**. The integrante's status changes to **Convertido** and a new member record appears in the Members module.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Converting an integrante **consumes one member slot** from your plan. Check that you have available capacity before converting. The member counter in the app header shows your current usage.
</Note>

## Filters

Use the Integration list filters to focus on specific segments of your pipeline:

- **Status** — Em andamento, Convertido, or Descartado
- **Congregação esperada** — filter by the target congregation
- **Mentor** — see all integrantes assigned to a specific mentor

Active filter chips appear at the top of the list. Click the **×** on a chip to remove it.

## PDF export

Export integrante data as PDF in two ways:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Individual integrante" icon="file-pdf" href="#">
    Open the integrante's detail view and click **Exportar PDF** to download their full profile.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Filtered list" icon="file-lines" href="#">
    Apply filters, then open the export menu and click **Exportar lista (PDF)**. Select which fields to include before downloading.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Public integration link

Flock can generate a **public link** that you share with visitors — no login required. Anyone who opens the link fills out a short form with their own contact details, and Flock automatically creates an integrante record on your behalf.

This is ideal for:
- Sharing after a service via WhatsApp or social media
- Embedding in your church website
- Printing as a QR code on bulletins or welcome cards

You can create multiple links (for example, one per congregation or one per outreach event), deactivate a link when it's no longer needed, and view all submissions attributed to each link.

For a full walkthrough, see [Public Links](/guides/public-links).

<Note>
  Integrantes do **not** count against your plan's member limit. They only consume a slot when they are converted to full members.
</Note>
