Congregations in Flock represent the physical locations or branches that make up your church — your main church building plus any satellite sites, daughter churches, or meeting points you operate. Every member, group, integration candidate, and calendar event can be tied to a specific congregation, giving you the ability to view and filter your entire operation by location. Whether you run a single site or a network of dozens, the Congregations module keeps your structure clear and consistent.

<Tip>
  Set up all your congregations **before** adding members. That way you can assign each new member to the right congregation during their initial registration, avoiding the need to go back and update records later.
</Tip>

## The Sede (headquarters)

Every church registered in Flock starts with a default congregation called **Sede**. The Sede represents your main church and is created automatically when you sign up — you do not need to create it yourself. All other congregations you add represent branches, satellite sites, or additional meeting points.

The Sede behaves exactly like any other congregation: you can set its address, assign a leader, and filter reports by it. The only difference is that it cannot be deleted, since it is the root of your church structure.

## Creating a congregation

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Congregations module">
    Click **Congregações** in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a new congregation">
    Click **Adicionar congregação**. The creation form opens.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the name">
    Type a unique name for this congregation. No two congregations under your church can share the same name — Flock validates this automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add the address">
    Enter the street address, neighbourhood, city, and state. City and state fields use data from the IBGE API, so you can search by typing a few letters and selecting from the dropdown. A fallback list is available if the API is temporarily unreachable.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assign a leader (optional)">
    Select a leader from the list of your existing members. If you haven't added members yet, leave this field blank — you can assign or update the leader at any time by editing the congregation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add contact info (optional)">
    Provide a phone number or email address for this congregation if applicable.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Salvar**. The congregation appears in the list immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Leader assignment

The leader (líder) of a congregation must be an existing member of your church. Only people already registered in the Members module appear in the leader selection dropdown.

You can change the assigned leader at any time by opening the congregation and clicking **Editar**. There is no limit to how many times you can reassign leadership.

<Note>
  If you have not yet added any members, the leader field will be empty. This is fine — create your congregations first, add your members, then come back to assign leaders.
</Note>

## Member count

The congregation list shows the **active member count** for each congregation at a glance. This count updates automatically whenever a member is added, transferred, activated, or deactivated. Inactive members are not included in this count.

## Batch creation

If you are onboarding and need to create several congregations at once, use the **batch creation** feature. Click the batch icon next to the "Add congregation" button, enter each congregation's details in the multi-row form, and save all records in a single operation.

## Protection against deletion

Flock prevents you from accidentally deleting a congregation that still has active members assigned to it. If you attempt to delete a congregation with members, you'll see a message explaining that you must first reassign or deactivate those members before the congregation can be removed.

This protection ensures you never lose member-congregation relationships through an accidental deletion.

## Unique names

Congregation names must be unique within your church. If you try to create or rename a congregation to a name that already exists, Flock will show a validation error before saving. This keeps your dropdown menus and filter lists clean and unambiguous.

## Exporting

You can export the congregation list as a PDF at any time. Apply a search filter if needed, then click **Exportar PDF**. The download includes each congregation's name, location, leader, and active member count.
