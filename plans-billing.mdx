Flock is built on the principle that every church — regardless of size — should have access to professional management tools. That's why you can start completely free and only pay as your congregation grows. There are no setup fees, no contracts, and no hidden charges. When your church is ready to move to a paid plan, the upgrade takes less than a minute and unlocks a higher member capacity while keeping all your existing data exactly as it is.

## What counts against your member limit

Your plan's member limit applies only to **active members**. Here's what that means in practice:

- **Active members** count toward your limit. Each time you add a new active member, the counter increments.
- **Inactive members** remain in the system for your historical records, but they do **not** count against your limit. You can deactivate a member at any time without losing their data.
- **Integrantes** (visitors and candidates in the Integration pipeline) are **not** members and do not count toward your limit — even if you have hundreds of them. Only when you convert an integrante to a formal member does the counter change.

The **member counter** is always visible in the top of the app header, showing you how many active members you have relative to your plan's limit (for example, "87 / 100"). When you approach your limit, Flock displays a warning. When you reach it, new member creation is blocked until you upgrade or deactivate existing members.

## How to upgrade your plan

Upgrading takes effect immediately and is handled securely through Stripe.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    Click **Configurações** in the sidebar (or navigate to `/settings`), then select the **Assinatura** (Subscription) tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a new plan">
    You'll see your current plan, your member count, and the available upgrade options. Click the plan you want to move to.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete payment">
    You'll be redirected to the Stripe checkout or Stripe customer portal to complete the payment. Stripe handles all payment processing — Flock never stores your card details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirmation">
    Once payment is confirmed, your new member limit is activated immediately. You'll see the updated limit reflected in the header counter right away.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## How to downgrade your plan

You can switch to a lower-capacity plan at any time, as long as your current number of active members is within the lower plan's limit. For example, to move from the 500-member plan to the 200-member plan, you must have 200 or fewer active members at the time of the change.

To downgrade, go to **Settings → Subscription** and choose a lower plan. The change is managed through the Stripe customer portal.

## How to cancel your subscription

If you need to cancel your paid subscription and return to the free plan (100-member limit):

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    Go to **Configurações → Assinatura**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access the billing portal">
    Click **Gerenciar assinatura** to open the Stripe customer portal.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Cancel your subscription">
    Inside the Stripe portal, select the option to cancel your subscription. Your paid access continues until the end of the current billing period, after which your account reverts to the free plan limits.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Cancelling your subscription does **not** delete your data. All your member records, congregations, groups, and history remain intact. If your active member count exceeds 100 after the cancellation takes effect, you won't be able to add new members until you either reactivate a paid plan or deactivate enough members to fall within the free limit.
</Note>

## Payment processing

All payments are processed securely by **Stripe**, a globally trusted payment platform. Flock never stores your credit card information. You can view your billing history, update your payment method, and manage your subscription at any time through the Stripe customer portal, accessible from **Settings → Subscription → Gerenciar assinatura**.

<Tip>
  Keep an eye on the **member counter in the app header** — it updates in real time whenever you add or deactivate members. This is the easiest way to know how much room you have before reaching your plan's limit.
</Tip>
